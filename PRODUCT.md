# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Janus is used by internal platform engineers, finance operators, and team leads during repeated operational workflows: user administration, billing review, usage analysis, model allocation, and catalog maintenance.

## Product Purpose

Janus is the company unified internal AI infrastructure administration console. It exists to make dense CRUD, tables, forms, and operational status visible and controllable without exposing backend complexity.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, precise, dependable. The interface should feel like a quiet command center for people who already know what they are doing.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-site drama, decorative gradients, oversized hero composition inside the app shell, novelty controls, and one-off visual overrides that drift from Ant Design tokens.

## Design Principles

- Density with order: show operational detail without sacrificing scanability.
- Familiar controls first: Ant Design and Pro Components are the primary interaction vocabulary.
- Token discipline: component appearance belongs in theme tokens; layout rhythm belongs in Tailwind spacing.
- Responsive structure: navigation adapts by breakpoint rather than squeezing desktop chrome onto mobile.
- Motion serves state: transitions should be fast, restrained, and never block work.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Keep keyboard-visible focus states, meaningful button labels, no color-only state communication, and respect reduced-motion preferences.
